How I Work

Dive into the data

When approaching any problems in the business, I will always refer to all the data available to me and what can ultimately be uncovered. Being data-driven helps uncover key insights and opportunities for key stakeholders.

Empathy and Communication

Coming from a sales background, I believe it is important to remain transparent and understanding with whoever I communicate with. Understanding the problems at the deepest level help establish credibility with the stakeholders to help find solutions to whatever the problem may be.

Persistence leads to results

"Nothing in the world can take the place of persistence". Being persistent, especially working with data, is important because you will run into problems and errors. Staying focused on the goal at-hand drives my efficiency and skills in manipulating and cleaning data, and gets better every day.

Passion for finding answers

Data can provide many insights into the problem at-hand. Cleaning, analyzing, and providing reccomendations is something that not only provides answers to the question at-hand, but provides a sense of accomplishment. The ability to feel accomplished while supporting key stakeholders is what keeps me coming back to data.
